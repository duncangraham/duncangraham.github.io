Internships

An internship with The Washington Post is about more than just real work experience. It’s an unrivaled opportunity to experience what goes into making The Washington Post one of the world’s leading news and information organizations. You’ll immerse yourself in our day-to-day operations. You’ll network with individuals at all levels. Most of all: You’ll get firsthand knowledge you won’t find in the classroom or lecture hall. Who knows? You may even go on to find a permanent home here.

Newsroom Internships

Eager to play a pivotal role in shaping the future of news media? We offer 12-week paid newsroom internships for

  • Reporters (Local, Financial, Sports, Style and Editorial desks)
  • Visual Journalists
  • Producers (Multiplatform and Interactivity)
  • Web Designers
