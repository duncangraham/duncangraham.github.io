About Surgical Associates

General & Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery in Buffalo, NY

Welcome to Surgical Associates of Western New York. We are one of Buffalo’s largest general surgery practices with convenient office locations in West Seneca, Kenmore and Derby.

Our practice offers a wide range of surgeries and procedures, including minimally invasive surgery, SILSTM (Single-Incision Laparoscopic Surgery), robotic-assisted surgery, and operations using the latest implant devices – to name just a few.

We also provide in-office hemorrhoid treatment, which takes only a few minutes and requires no anesthesia.

Working with Your General Surgeon

Our general surgeons have operating privileges at most hospitals and outpatient treatment centers throughout Western New York, including Mercy Hospital, Kenmore Mercy, and the Center for Ambulatory Surgery (an outpatient center) located in our West Seneca office.

For more than 35 years, our surgical practice has maintained its commitment to patient satisfaction and achieving the best clinical outcomes in the region. We know surgery is a significant event for you and your family, and our dedicated team will offer you our best services and support every step of the way.
