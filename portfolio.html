I've had the good luck to work on a wide array of awesome projects with an equally large amount of wonderful people. Here are some of my favorites.

  • The Aegis Experiment

    I spent 5 days at CERN as a designer and front end developer in the Tribeca Film Insititute's Storymatter Hackathon. During that time I collaborated with scientists, filmographers, and a backend developer to make an educational webapp focused on an ongoing experiment studying the gravitional effects on antimatter. Check out the alpha version!

  • News App Concept

    It's time to marry news, current technology, and our mobile habits. Above is my vision for the future: an app that balances personal choice, technological prowess and editorial savvy. The main page would be a feed of the news items deigned by editors to be neccessary for cultural literacy.

    Users would choose how much time they wanted to invest on each item, and would subsequently be served atomic slices of news related to the story and personalized to their knowledge of the subject & context. Meta information, such as relevant persons and orgaizations, would allow users to educate themselves on the context.
